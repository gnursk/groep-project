willem the first

Portrait of Willem 1, Prince of Orange, Adriaen Thomasz. The face of the Dutch revolt against Spanish rule His resistance in 1569 led to the start of the Eighty Years' War and formed the basis for the later independent republic of the Netherlands, Willem van Oranje doesn't exactly look like a fighter's boss here, his strength therefore lay more in clever diplomatic play than in the battlefield.

Vincent van Gogh

They say – and I like to believe it – that it is difficult to know yourself – but it is not easy either to paint yourself', Van Gogh once wrote to his brother Theo. In this self-portrait he portrayed himself as a decent citizen with an elegant jacket and felt hat.

Rembrandt

Rembrandt Harmenszoon van Rijn (Leiden, 15 July 1606 – Amsterdam, October 4, 1669) was a Dutch painter, etcher and draftsman. He is generally regarded as one of the greatest painters and etchers in European art, and as the most important Dutch master of the 17th century.
